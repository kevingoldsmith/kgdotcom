Fail Safe, Fail Smart, Succeed
Presenting "Fail Safe, Fail Smart... Succeed! at JAX 2017 in Mainz, Germany
Description
A talk on creating a fail-safe culture, not by eliminating the chances of failure, but by expecting failures as an inevitability in an innovative organization and minimizing the impact of those failures when they happen.
